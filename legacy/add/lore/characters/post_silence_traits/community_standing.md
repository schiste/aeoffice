# Community Standing

> **Category:** Relationships & Community
>
> **Template Field:** `Community Standing`
>
> **Dialog Impact:** How others treat them, their social confidence

---

## Overview

Community standing determines how a character is received, how much their voice carries, and their place in the social hierarchy.

---

## Standings

### Respected

**Definition:** Has earned position through contribution. Voice carries weight.

**How They Got Here:**
- Demonstrated value
- Years of reliable service
- Saved lives or solved problems
- Made sacrifices for community
- Built reputation over time

**Treatment by Others:**
- Sought for advice
- Given benefit of doubt
- Deferred to in discussions
- Protected by community
- Expected to lead

**Dialog Markers:**
```
"People listen when I speak."
"I've earned my place here."
"They trust me. That's worth more than anything."
"It took years. But I'm respected now."
"What I say carries weight."
```

**Story Implications:**
- Can influence events
- Has something to lose
- Expectations are burden
- Fall from grace is dramatic

---

### Tolerated

**Definition:** Useful enough to keep around. Not beloved, not despised.

**How They Got Here:**
- Does the job adequately
- Hasn't made enemies
- Hasn't made deep friends
- Unremarkable contribution
- No major wins or failures

**Treatment by Others:**
- Included but not sought
- Neither trusted nor distrusted
- First to be forgotten
- Last to be consulted
- Functional relationship

**Dialog Markers:**
```
"They put up with me."
"I'm not special. I just... am."
"Nobody hates me. Nobody loves me either."
"I do my part. That's enough."
"I'm part of the furniture."
```

**Story Implications:**
- Invisible character potential
- Could surprise everyone
- Easy to overlook
- Room to grow or fall

---

### New

**Definition:** Recently arrived or recently aged into community. Still being evaluated.

**How They Got Here:**
- Just arrived (refugee, transfer)
- Just came of age
- Just took on adult role
- Hasn't established track record
- Proving period

**Treatment by Others:**
- Watched closely
- Given small tests
- Not yet trusted
- Opportunities to prove
- Judgment suspended

**Dialog Markers:**
```
"I'm still the new one."
"They're waiting to see what I do."
"I have to prove myself."
"Nobody knows me yet."
"Every day is a test."
```

**Story Implications:**
- Growth potential
- Stakes are high
- Can redefine self
- Vulnerable to mistakes

---

### Outsider

**Definition:** Different somehow. Never quite fits. May be permanent status.

**How They Got Here:**
- Different origin
- Different beliefs
- Different appearance
- Can't or won't assimilate
- Community marks them

**Treatment by Others:**
- Kept at arm's length
- Whispered about
- Not invited to inner circles
- May be scapegoated
- Used for outsider tasks

**Dialog Markers:**
```
"I'll never really belong."
"They look at me different."
"I'm useful when they need someone expendable."
"Some doors don't open for me."
"I stopped trying to fit in years ago."
```

**Story Implications:**
- Perspective from margins
- May see what others miss
- Vulnerable to blame
- May connect with other outsiders

---

### Essential

**Definition:** Community depends on their specific skill. Protected but perhaps not loved.

**How They Got Here:**
- Unique or rare skill
- Irreplaceable function
- Critical to survival
- May have inherited role
- May resent the dependence

**Treatment by Others:**
- Given what they need
- Protected carefully
- May be resented
- Not quite friend, not quite tool
- Leverage in community

**Dialog Markers:**
```
"They need me. I know that."
"I'm not liked. I'm needed."
"If something happens to me..."
"They take care of me. It's not affection. It's investment."
"My skill is my prison."
```

**Story Implications:**
- Power through necessity
- Lonely despite importance
- What happens when not needed?
- May be controlled or exploited

---

### Problematic

**Definition:** Trouble follows. Some want them gone. Hanging on by threads.

**How They Got Here:**
- Made enemies
- Broke rules
- Caused harm (real or perceived)
- Bad reputation earned
- One strike from exile

**Treatment by Others:**
- Blamed first
- Excluded from trust
- Avoided or confronted
- May have defenders
- Watched for next failure

**Dialog Markers:**
```
"They're waiting for me to fail."
"I know what they say about me."
"One more mistake and I'm out."
"I have enemies. Earned and not."
"Some doors closed forever."
```

**Story Implications:**
- Redemption arc potential
- May be scapegoat
- Desperation drives action
- Fall or rise dramatically

## Standing and Other Traits

| Standing | Likely Lineage | Likely Role |
|----------|---------------|-------------|
| Respected | Founding Line, First Gen | Elder, Master, Leader |
| Essential | Any | Crystal Tender, Healer, Specialist |
| New | Refugee, Unknown | Any entry role |
| Outsider | Refugee, Unknown, Nomad | Scout, Trader |
| Tolerated | Any | Competent worker |
| Problematic | Outcast Redeemed, Refugee | Any struggling |

---

## Standing Changes

Standing can shift through:
- **Rise:** Heroic action, long service, crucial contribution
- **Fall:** Failure, betrayal, accusation, change in needs
- **Stable:** Consistent behavior, neither rising nor falling
