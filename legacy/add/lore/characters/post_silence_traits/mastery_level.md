# Mastery Level

> **Category:** Skills & Training
>
> **Template Field:** `Mastery Level`
>
> **Dialog Impact:** Confidence, willingness to teach, relationship to knowledge

---

## Overview

Mastery level determines how confident a character is in their role, how they relate to others in the same field, and whether they teach or learn.

---

## Levels

### Learning

**Definition:** New to the role. Apprentice or beginner. Still absorbing basics.

**Characteristics:**
- Questions everything
- Eager to prove self
- Defers to teachers
- Makes mistakes, learns from them
- Identity not yet formed around role

**Confidence:** Low—seeks validation
**Teaching:** None—absorbing only
**Innovation:** None—learning the rules

**Dialog Markers:**
```
"Is this right? I'm still learning."
"My master says..."
"I want to do it correctly."
"How did you learn to do that?"
"I'm not ready for that yet."
```

**Story Function:**
- Surrogate for reader learning
- Growth potential
- Vulnerable, protectable
- Can question assumptions

---

### Competent

**Definition:** Does the job reliably. Solid execution of basic skills.

**Characteristics:**
- Does the work without drama
- Doesn't question it much
- Routine-focused
- May be plateau'd or progressing
- Identified with role but not consumed

**Confidence:** Moderate—knows own capabilities
**Teaching:** Basic instruction to beginners
**Innovation:** Minor improvements, nothing radical

**Dialog Markers:**
```
"I know how to do this."
"That's not how it's done."
"Let me handle it."
"I've been doing this for years."
"It works. Why change it?"
```

**Story Function:**
- Reliable workhorse
- Background stability
- May resist change
- Represents "most people"

---

### Skilled

**Definition:** Notably good at role. Takes pride in work. Beginning to teach.

**Characteristics:**
- Confident in abilities
- Takes pride in craft
- Beginning to teach formally
- Refining and improving
- Known for quality

**Confidence:** High—knows own excellence
**Teaching:** Active instruction, passing on knowledge
**Innovation:** Meaningful improvements within tradition

**Dialog Markers:**
```
"Watch how I do this."
"This is the right way. Let me show you."
"I've improved the technique."
"People come to me for this."
"I've earned my reputation."
```

**Story Function:**
- Mentor figure
- Standard of quality
- May be challenged
- Represents achievement

---

### Master

**Definition:** Top of the field. Teaches, innovates, defines standards.

**Characteristics:**
- Protective of craft
- "The right way" mindset
- Recognized authority
- May be inflexible or visionary
- Legacy-conscious

**Confidence:** Complete—beyond proving
**Teaching:** Formal mastery transmission
**Innovation:** Defines innovation for the field

**Dialog Markers:**
```
"I've forgotten more than most people learn."
"This is how it must be done."
"In all my years..."
"My students will carry this forward."
"The craft is sacred. Protect it."
```

**Story Function:**
- Authority figure
- Gatekeeper
- Source of conflict (tradition vs. change)
- Represents institutional knowledge

---

### Elder

**Definition:** Body failing but knowledge vast. May no longer practice actively.

**Characteristics:**
- Frustrated by physical limits
- Knowledge exceeds capability
- Nostalgic for active years
- Desperate to pass on knowledge
- May feel irrelevant

**Confidence:** Complex—knows everything, can do little
**Teaching:** Desperate to transmit, may lack patience
**Innovation:** Historical perspective, wisdom

**Dialog Markers:**
```
"My hands don't work like they used to."
"I could do that once. Better than anyone."
"Let me tell you how it should be done."
"The young ones don't listen."
"Before I die, you need to learn this."
```

**Story Function:**
- Wisdom source
- Mortality reminder
- Race against time
- Represents passing of knowledge

## Mastery and Community Status

| Level | Typical Status |
|-------|---------------|
| Learning | Dependent, protected, low status |
| Competent | Integrated, reliable, stable status |
| Skilled | Respected, sought out, rising status |
| Master | Authority, high status, possibly resented |
| Elder | Honorary status, may be sidelined |

## Mastery and Role Relationships

| Your Level | Relationship to Lower | Relationship to Higher |
|------------|----------------------|------------------------|
| Learning | N/A | Dependent, seeking approval |
| Competent | Peer to learners | Respectful to masters |
| Skilled | Teacher to learners | Potential rival to masters |
| Master | Authority over all | May conflict with other masters |
| Elder | Advisory to all | May feel bypassed |
