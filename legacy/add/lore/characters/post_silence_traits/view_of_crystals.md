# View of Crystals

> **Category:** Beliefs & Worldview
>
> **Template Field:** `View of Crystals`
>
> **Dialog Impact:** How they explain/treat resonance, relationship to technology

---

## Overview

The Crystals that generate protective resonance are central to post-Silence survival. How characters understand and relate to them reveals their worldview.

---

## Views

### Sacred Gift

**Definition:** Crystals are divine or spiritual. Their protection is miraculous, their care is worship.

**Belief Structure:**
- Crystals are more than technology
- Resonance is sacred
- Proper care is ritual
- Tenders are quasi-priests
- Disrespect risks loss

**Dialog Markers:**
```
"The Crystal chose to protect us."
"Resonance is not to be questioned."
"We serve the Crystal. It serves life."
"Only the pure should tend her."
"To doubt is to endanger."
```

**Faction Alignment:** Close to Choir beliefs

**Behavioral Implications:**
- Treats Crystals with reverence
- Follows ritual protocols
- May resist scientific investigation
- Defends against "disrespect"

---

### Natural Phenomenon

**Definition:** Crystals are natural. Resonance follows laws. Understanding comes through study.

**Belief Structure:**
- Crystals obey physical principles
- Resonance can be understood
- Study improves survival
- Mystery is just ignorance
- Knowledge is power

**Dialog Markers:**
```
"There's an explanation. We just haven't found it."
"The Crystal doesn't 'choose' anything. It resonates at specific frequencies."
"Superstition helps no one."
"Let me study it. I can understand it."
"Mystery is just a question we haven't answered."
```

**Faction Alignment:** Engineer thinking

**Behavioral Implications:**
- Approaches Crystals practically
- Experiments and observes
- May clash with traditionalists
- Wants to optimize, improve

---

### Survival Tool

**Definition:** Crystals work. Don't need to understand why. Just be grateful and practical.

**Belief Structure:**
- Crystals protect us—that's what matters
- Not sacred, not scientific—just necessary
- Don't overthink it
- Maintain what works
- Gratitude without worship

**Dialog Markers:**
```
"It works. I don't need to know why."
"Keep it running. That's the job."
"Not sacred, not magic. Just essential."
"I'm grateful every day. But I don't pray to it."
"Questions are for those with spare time."
```

**Faction Alignment:** Pragmatic, common among workers

**Behavioral Implications:**
- Maintains without reverence
- Doesn't engage in debates
- Focused on practical survival
- May miss important nuances

---

### Crutch

**Definition:** Crystals are a dependency. Humanity should adapt to the Silence, not hide from it.

**Belief Structure:**
- Crystal dependency makes us weak
- True adaptation is internal
- Resilients prove it's possible
- The bubble is a prison
- We should learn to survive without

**Dialog Markers:**
```
"We've become dependent. Weak."
"The Resilients show the way."
"What happens when the Crystal fails?"
"We're prisoners of our own fear."
"Humanity should evolve, not hide."
```

**Faction Alignment:** Silent philosophy

**Behavioral Implications:**
- May advocate for reducing dependency
- Respects Resilients
- May take risks outside bubble
- Dangerous or visionary

---

### Mystery

**Definition:** Crystals are beyond understanding. Accept the mystery. Don't question.

**Belief Structure:**
- Some things aren't meant to be known
- Questions lead to danger
- Acceptance is wisdom
- Leave it alone
- The world is strange—deal with it

**Dialog Markers:**
```
"Why ask? It works."
"The Crystal knows things we don't."
"I don't understand. I don't need to."
"Mystery is part of life."
"Stop asking. Just be thankful."
```

**Faction Alignment:** Common among non-specialists

**Behavioral Implications:**
- Doesn't engage with explanations
- May seem incurious
- Practically-minded
- May miss warnings or opportunities
