## The Conspiracy: "Operation Lullaby"

### The Telegram Group: "Sleepless in Decibels"

**Founded:** June 21st, 2026 — during [Fête de la Musique](thesaurus.md#fête-de-la-musique) (France's national music day, where amateur musicians flood every street corner until 2am). See [timeline.md](timeline.md#2026) for the founding night.

**Original Topic:** "Support group for parents living above bars, next to concert venues, and below amateur DJs"

**Membership:** Started as 42 exhausted parents venting about bass vibrations, 3am drum solos, and the neighbor's kid learning trumpet. Within two years, it had grown to ~2,000 members across 30 countries.

**The Pivot:** By 2028, the group had attracted an unusual concentration of high-performing professionals — people whose careers demanded focus and whose children demanded sleep. The venting turned to scheming. The scheming turned to planning.

### The Plan (As Designed)

**Goal:** "A quieter world for everyone" — the conspirators genuinely believed they were improving civilization.

**Component 1: The Dampening Network (Satellites)** — See [thesaurus.md](thesaurus.md#the-filter)
- 340 satellites disguised as atmospheric research platforms
- Launched between 2030-2033 via multiple commercial providers (see [timeline.md](timeline.md#2030) for launch schedule)
- Designed to emit a targeted counter-frequency field
- **Intended effect:** Suppress sounds above 75dB in open air (concerts, clubs, construction, traffic)
- **Intended range:** 20Hz-8kHz (bass through mid-range — the "annoying" frequencies)
- Think "global noise-canceling AirPods" — whispers and conversation would remain untouched

**Component 2: The Hush (Virus)** — See [thesaurus.md](thesaurus.md#the-hush--hush-2)
- A synthetic retrovirus targeting the cochlear nerve
- **Intended effect:** Induce mild hyperacusis (sound sensitivity) in infected individuals — loud noises would cause headaches, encouraging quieter behavior
- **Transmission:** Airborne, highly contagious (by design — they wanted global coverage within months)
- **Lethality:** None. The virus was engineered to be completely benign except for the sensitivity effect.
- **Delivery:** Released at 12 major music festivals simultaneously, summer 2034 (see [timeline.md](timeline.md#2034) for distribution)

### What Went Wrong

**The Virus (Mutation — October 8-10, 2034):** See [timeline.md](timeline.md#the-mutation-october-8-10-2034) for details.
1. The original virus was stable in lab conditions
2. In the wild, it encountered an unexpected interaction with a common rhinovirus strain
3. The hybrid mutation (later called "[The Silence](thesaurus.md#the-silence--the-deadly-silent)" or "Hush-2") altered the virus's behavior dramatically
4. Hush-2 lodges in the brainstem's auditory processing centers and becomes **active when not suppressed by sound vibrations**
5. **The mechanism:** Sound vibrations (transmitted through bone conduction and inner ear) keep the virus in a dormant state. In silence, the virus activates and begins attacking neural tissue
6. **New effect:** Without continuous sound exposure, the active virus causes cascading neural damage: hallucinations → motor loss → coma → death
7. **Timeline:** 4 hours for most humans (the virus activates within minutes of silence, but lethal damage accumulates over ~4 hours)
8. **Why sound works:** The virus was originally designed to respond to acoustic stimulation (to induce hyperacusis). The mutation inverted this — instead of activating in response to sound, it now activates in the *absence* of sound. Continuous vibration keeps it dormant.
9. **The tragedy:** The mutation happened days before [The Filter](thesaurus.md#the-filter) activated. Nobody noticed because symptoms looked like "festival flu" and normal sound was still propagating.

**The Satellites (Cascading Failure — October 14, 2034):** See [timeline.md](timeline.md#the-cascade-october-14-2034) for hour-by-hour account.
1. A solar storm in October 2034 damaged 23 satellites' calibration systems
2. Automated repair protocols attempted to compensate by boosting output
3. The boosted signal created harmonic interference with undamaged satellites
4. Within 72 hours, the network had amplified itself into a full-spectrum dampening field
5. **Result:** Near-total suppression of airborne sound propagation (20Hz-15kHz)

The satellites couldn't be shut down — the kill codes required ground-based radio transmission, which no longer propagated.

**The Conspirators:**
- Four of the five Sounding Five died in the first wave
- @NapTimeNinja (Marc Delacroix) survived — he had been secretly supporting [The Unplugged](#the-unplugged-the-survivors-who-caused-it) since 2032 through an anonymous account (@QuietDad_FR), ensuring they had the right supplies, the right cave depths, and the drums that would save everyone. He formally joined in September 2034, evacuating with his family to the Ardèche caves when the cascade began.
- @WhiteNoise_Mom's last message to the group: "It wasn't supposed to do this. I'm so sorry. Tell my kids I—" (message incomplete)
- The Telegram group's server logs survived on a bunker backup. They are one of the most dangerous documents in the post-Silence world.

### The Death Toll (REVISED)

> **LORE REVISION v2:** Surface = death. No exceptions. Only underground survivors.

See [timeline.md](timeline.md#the-collapse-2034-2035) for full collapse timeline.

**Day 1:** ~7.6 billion dead (everyone on surface)
**Week 1:** ~7.9 billion dead (continued attrition)
**Month 1:** ~7.95 billion dead
**Year 1:** ~7.999+ billion dead
**Stable underground population (Year 1):** ~120,000 survivors worldwide

**Why so few survived?** The Filter is absolute. Surface = death within 4 hours. The ONLY survivors were those already underground when The Silence began:
- Underground cave systems (natural insulation from satellites)
- Metro systems (Moscow, Tokyo, São Paulo, London)
- Deep mines (Mponeng, various)
- Prepper bunkers (boosted by 2028-2034 survivalism trend)
- **[The Unplugged](#the-unplugged-the-survivors-who-caused-it) caves** (see below)

> **REMOVED from old lore:** Industrial facilities, coastal areas, "sound shifts" — all surface-based survival was impossible. No amount of sound protects from The Filter on surface. Only depth (rock/earth) provides protection.

## The Unplugged: The Survivors Who Caused It

See [thesaurus.md](thesaurus.md#the-unplugged) for quick reference. See [timeline.md](timeline.md#2031) through [timeline.md](timeline.md#2034) for detailed preparation timeline.

### Origin

A splinter group within [Sleepless in Decibels](#the-telegram-group-sleepless-in-decibels) that took "what if something goes wrong?" seriously.

**Founded:** December 2031, as a private sub-channel called "The Unplugged" (the name was meant to be ironic — exhausted parents who just wanted to unplug from the noise)

**Founding member:** @UndergroundDad (real name: Henri Marchand), a civil engineer from Lyon

**Initial purpose:** "Just in case" preparation. Survivalist planning disguised as a hobby.

### Key Members

See [naming_conventions.md](naming_conventions.md#the-conspiracy-historical) for codename patterns.

| Codename | Real Role | Contribution to The Unplugged |
|----------|-----------|------------------------------|
| **@UndergroundDad** | Civil Engineer, Lyon | Cave surveys, structural assessments |
| **@CaveMapper** | Geologist, Grenoble | Identified viable cave systems across Europe |
| **@SporeMom** | Mycologist, University of Bordeaux | Mushroom cultivation systems, sustainable food |
| **@DrumCircleDad** | Retired Military Logistics Officer | Evacuation protocols, supply chain planning |
| **@NurseMidnight** | ER Nurse, Marseille | Medical supplies, first aid training |
| **@VetDad** | Veterinarian, rural Provence | Animal husbandry, alternative medicine |
| **@GeneratorGuy** | Electrical Engineer, Germany | Power systems, hand-crank technology |

### What They Built (2032-2034)

The Unplugged established a network of prepared cave shelters:

**Primary Sites:**
- **Ardèche Cave Complex (France)** — Limestone system, capacity ~200
- **Cantabrian Cave (Spain)** — Near Santander, capacity ~150
- **Harz Mountain System (Germany)** — Former mining tunnels, capacity ~100
- **Moravian Karst (Czech Republic)** — Natural caves, capacity ~80

**Standard Cache Contents:**
- Dried mushroom substrate and spore cultures (years of food potential)
- Water filtration systems (sand, charcoal, UV — all DIY-able)
- Hand tools (no power dependency)
- Medical supplies (antibiotics, surgical tools, reference books)
- Seed banks (vegetables that grow in low light)
- **Drums and percussion instruments** — included for "psychological well-being"
- Hand-written manuals on everything from mushroom cultivation to basic surgery

**The Irony:** They included drums because rhythm is calming. They had no idea the drums would be the only thing keeping them alive.

### The Activation (October 14, 2034)

See [timeline.md](timeline.md#october-14-15--the-unplugged-evacuation) for detailed evacuation account.

When [The Filter](thesaurus.md#the-filter) activated, the The Unplugged evacuation protocol triggered:

1. Members received pre-arranged signals (phone trees, since internet was failing)
2. Families converged on nearest prepared caves within hours
3. The drum circles started immediately — initially for morale, then for survival
4. By sheer luck, they had exactly what was needed: underground shelter (Filter-proof) and continuous sound generation (Hush-proof)

**Survival rate:** ~85% of The Unplugged members and their families survived the first month (compared to ~5% global average)

### The Guilt They Don't Know They Carry

**Critical fact:** The Unplugged members were *also* members of [Sleepless in Decibels](#the-telegram-group-sleepless-in-decibels). They knew [The Sounding Five](#key-conspirators-the-sounding-five). They saw the "what if we turned off music?" jokes. Some of them laughed.

More critically: @NapTimeNinja secretly guided The Unplugged from the beginning through an anonymous account (@QuietDad_FR). He suggested "continuous noise" for psychological well-being and funded drums "to keep morale up." He recommended cave depths that would block satellite signals. He funded supplies. He ensured they would survive what he was helping to create. When he told Henri "Just focus on your caves. Whatever happens, you'll be fine," it wasn't a dismissal — it was a promise he'd spent two years keeping.

But they didn't know about the private channel. They didn't know about the satellites or the virus. They thought they were just paranoid parents with too much time and anxiety.

**What the survivors believe (The Official Story):**
- "We were just prepared. Lucky, really."
- "The Silence came from nowhere. Some kind of natural disaster or weapon."
- "Our grandparents were smart enough to stock caves. We owe them everything."
- "The old Telegram group was just a support community. Nothing more."

**What the survivors don't know:**
- Their "support community" created the apocalypse
- [The Sounding Five](#key-conspirators-the-sounding-five) were fellow members
- One of the Sounding Five — Marc Delacroix — survived with them and led the caves for decades
- [The Archive](thesaurus.md#the-archive) has proof

### The Artifacts

The Unplugged caves contain items that could reveal the truth — if anyone knew what to look for:

**Innocuous artifacts (survivors have these, don't understand significance):**
- Telegram-branded merchandise (stickers, mugs) from group meetups
- Photos from the 2031 Five Year Anniversary meetup in Paris
- @UndergroundDad's original "just in case" planning documents (mentions "Sleepless in Decibels" by name)
- Hand-written journals referencing "the group," "the plan," "the satellites" (second-hand gossip, not direct knowledge)

**Dangerous artifacts (hidden, lost, or destroyed):**
- A partial printout of Telegram chat logs (water-damaged, fragments readable)
- @DrumCircleDad's "activation protocol" document (references "if the main plan goes wrong")
- Letters between The Unplugged members speculating about "what Marc and the others are really doing"
- The @QuietDad_FR account history — if recovered, would show Marc was guiding The Unplugged's preparations from the start

**The smoking gun (location unknown):**
- @UndergroundDad's encrypted hard drive, containing his private correspondence with @NapTimeNinja
- In 2032, Henri (UndergroundDad) asked Marc (NapTimeNinja) directly: "Are you actually doing something? Should I be more worried?"
- Marc's reply: "Just focus on your caves. Whatever happens, you'll be fine."
- Henri didn't understand it as a promise. Marc had spent months as @QuietDad_FR making sure the promise would hold.
- Henri died in 2090, never knowing that Marc was both the architect of their salvation and the engineer of the apocalypse.

## The Crystals: "Resonance Anomalies"

See [thesaurus.md](thesaurus.md#crystals--resonance-anomalies) for quick reference. See [timeline.md](timeline.md#the-crystal-discovery-era-2039-2100) for discovery history.

### Origin

The [Crystals](thesaurus.md#crystals--resonance-anomalies) were **not part of the conspiracy**. They are an unintended emergent phenomenon.

**The Science (As Best Understood):**
1. The satellite dampening field works by generating destructive interference patterns
2. These patterns are not uniform — they create standing waves in the atmosphere
3. At certain geographic points, the standing waves produce nodes of **constructive** interference
4. Over time (months to years), these nodes began to affect local geology
5. Specific mineral formations (high quartz/silicon content) began to resonate with the standing waves
6. The resonance created a feedback loop: the minerals amplified local vibration while partially canceling the dampening field

**The Result:** Naturally-occurring "Crystals" that create localized [bubbles](thesaurus.md#the-bubble) where sound can propagate normally — but only when fed acoustic energy (the Crystal couples vibration into the local field, overpowering the satellite interference).

### Crystal Formation

- Crystals formed at standing wave nodes over 50-200 years post-Silence
- They are **rare** — perhaps 200-400 worldwide
- They cannot be artificially created (the conditions are too specific)
- They *can* be moved, but this is extremely dangerous (the Crystal must be kept vibrating during transport or its field collapses)
- A Crystal separated from its original node is less efficient but still functional

### Crystal Distribution (Current Era — ~300 years post-Silence)

| Status | Count | Notes |
|--------|-------|-------|
| **Controlled by settlements** | ~80 | The basis of surviving human civilization |
| **Contested/fought over** | ~30 | Active conflicts, unstable regions |
| **Undiscovered** | ~100+ | In remote locations, underground, underwater |
| **Dormant/damaged** | ~40 | Crystals that stopped resonating (cause unknown) |
| **Destroyed** | ~20 | Shattered in wars, accidents, or by "[The Silent](thesaurus.md#the-silent)" faction |

### The Crystal Network Theory

Some scholars believe the Crystals are slowly synchronizing — that if enough of them were connected via relay systems, they could generate a field strong enough to permanently counter the satellites.

This is the basis of "[The Resonants](thesaurus.md#the-resonants)" faction's ideology.

Whether it's actually possible is deliberately left ambiguous.

## The Hero's Resistance

See [thesaurus.md](thesaurus.md#resilients) for quick reference.

### Timeline Context

The game takes place **approximately 300 years** after [The Deadly Silent](thesaurus.md#the-silence--the-deadly-silent) (the initial die-off event, 2034-2035).

At this point:
- Humanity has stabilized at ~2 million people worldwide (underground-only survival model)
- The [Hush-2](thesaurus.md#the-hush--hush-2) virus is endemic — every human carries it
- The 4-hour death window is universal... except for rare mutations

### The Resistance Mutation

**Prevalence:** Approximately 1 in 50,000 people
**Effect:** 24-hour tolerance to silence (instead of 4 hours)
**Cause:** A secondary mutation in the Hush-2 virus itself

Over 300 years, the virus continued to evolve. In some lineages, a variant emerged (Hush-2.7r) that has a slower onset. Carriers of this variant have 24 hours before brainstem shutdown begins.

**Key point:** The Hero is not "immune." They *will* die in silence. They just have more time.

### Why This Matters Narratively

See [tone_guide.md](tone_guide.md) for how to write the Hero.

- The Hero is not "chosen" or special in a mystical sense
- They won a genetic lottery that makes them useful, not heroic
- Their extended tolerance makes them ideal for exploration, scouting, and Crystal recovery
- Many "[Resilients](thesaurus.md#resilients)" are used as expendable scouts by settlements — the Hero's journey can explore this exploitation

### The Hero's Specific Origin

Left deliberately vague for player projection, but possible hooks:
- Escaped from a settlement that used Resilients as slave labor
- Was a scout who found a dormant Crystal and claimed it
- Is searching for answers about why they're different
- Has family/loved ones with normal 4-hour tolerance (motivation to expand [the bubble](thesaurus.md#the-bubble))

## The Survivor Cave: Descendants of the The Unplugged

See [thesaurus.md](thesaurus.md#the-survivor-cave) for quick reference. See [specifications.md](../mechanics/specifications.md#341-crew-intake-survivor-cave--vibes-draft) for recruitment mechanics.

### Location

The [Survivor Cave](thesaurus.md#the-survivor-cave) near the Hero's [Base](thesaurus.md#the-base) is one of the original [The Unplugged](#the-unplugged-the-survivors-who-caused-it) sites — likely a branch of the Ardèche Cave Complex or a similar prepared shelter.

### What They Know (The Official Story)

The survivors have passed down a sanitized version of their history:

> "Our ancestors were wise and prepared. When the Silence came, they had already built shelters and stored supplies. They survived because they were ready. We honor their foresight by maintaining the drums, growing the mushrooms, and waiting for rescue."

They believe:
- [The Silence](thesaurus.md#the-silence--the-deadly-silent) was a natural disaster or unknown weapon
- Their ancestors were unrelated to its cause
- The "old Telegram group" was just a parenting support community
- The drums were included for psychological comfort (true, but incomplete)

### What They Have (Artifacts)

**Common items (every survivor family has some):**
- Mushroom cultivation knowledge (passed down orally and in hand-written manuals)
- Drum-making traditions (critical survival skill)
- Stories about "the founders" — heroic figures who saved everyone
- Faded photographs of pre-Silence life
- The occasional piece of Telegram merchandise (treated as ancestral relics)

**Rare items (a few families possess, significance unknown):**
- @UndergroundDad's original planning journals (mention "[Sleepless in Decibels](#the-telegram-group-sleepless-in-decibels)")
- Letters referencing "the main group" and "the plan"
- A water-damaged tablet with partial chat logs (unreadable without tech)
- The "activation protocol" document (references "if the main plan goes wrong")

**The smoking gun (one family has it, doesn't know what it is):**
- A small encrypted hard drive, passed down as "grandfather Henri's keepsake"
- It requires a password no one knows
- It contains the correspondence between @UndergroundDad and @NapTimeNinja
- If decrypted, it would prove the survivors' ancestors knew the conspirators personally

### The Revelation (Player Discovery)

The player can discover the truth through:
1. **Artifacts found in the Survivor Cave** — journals, letters, photographs
2. **Artifacts found in expeditions** — [Archive](thesaurus.md#the-archive) caches, ESA facility records, Telegram server fragments
3. **NPC dialogue** — older survivors who remember "strange stories" from their grandparents
4. **The encrypted hard drive** — a late-game puzzle requiring Tech research to decrypt

**Narrative payoff:**
- The people the Hero is rescuing are descendants of the people who caused the apocalypse
- They don't know, and telling them could destroy their community
- The Hero must decide: reveal the truth, or let them keep their comforting myth?

### The Telegram Logs

The complete logs of "[Sleepless in Decibels](#the-telegram-group-sleepless-in-decibels)" exist in exactly three locations:
1. A hardened server bunker in Switzerland ([Archive](thesaurus.md#the-archive)-controlled)
2. A partially corrupted backup in an abandoned ESA facility
3. Fragmented copies distributed among Archive safehouses

The logs contain:
- The full conspiracy, names, and plans
- Technical specifications for the satellites
- The original [Hush](thesaurus.md#the-hush--hush-2) virus design (potentially useful for creating treatments)
- The identities of [The Sounding Five](#key-conspirators-the-sounding-five) (whose descendants definitely exist — Marc's lineage continued in the caves)
- **References to [The Unplugged](#the-unplugged-the-survivors-who-caused-it)** — proving the connection between survivors and conspirators

**The Archive's Policy:** They sell fragments, never the whole truth. The complete logs are their ultimate leverage.

## The Name: "The Deadly Silent"

See [thesaurus.md](thesaurus.md#the-silence--the-deadly-silent) for definition.

The event is called "The Deadly Silent" in common parlance.

The name has two origins:
1. **Literal:** The silence that kills
2. **Dark humor:** Survivors developed gallows humor quickly. "Silent but deadly" was already a fart joke. The inversion — "Deadly Silent" — became the name because laughing at the apocalypse was the only way to survive it.

Some settlements consider the name disrespectful. [The Archive](thesaurus.md#the-archive) records both names: "The Silence Event" (formal) and "The Deadly Silent" (common).
