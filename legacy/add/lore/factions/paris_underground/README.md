## The Factions

### 1. La Dalle Éternelle ("The Eternal Slab")
**Territory:** West — La Défense complex
**Population:** ~20,000-24,000
**Government:** Conseil d'Administration (corporate board)
**Character:** Preppers who became what they parodied — corporate culture as governance

### 2. Le Palais Profond ("The Deep Palace")
**Territory:** Centre-Nord — Élysée → Concorde → Grand Palais
**Population:** ~5,000-7,000
**Government:** Constitutional Republic (43rd President)
**Character:** The French Republic that never ended — bureaucracy as religion

## Key Historical Events

### Year 1-3: The Dying Time
- Day 1: ~50,000-100,000 underground when The Silence hits
- Week 1: Chaos, panic, resource wars, ~40% die
- Month 3: First organized groups emerge
- Year 1: ~10,000-15,000 survivors
- Years 2-3: Le Palais Profond still sealed (the Door Problem)

### Year 78-82: The Great War
- Cause: La Dalle Éternelle tried to control Le Carrefour
- All factions dragged in
- ~5,000 dead over 4 years
- Le Grand Nord strikes against the war itself (Year 79)
- Ended by Treaty of Platform A (Year 82)
- Le Carrefour established as neutral

### Year 156: The Second Treaty
- Formalized all territorial borders
- Established trade routes
- Les Canaux recognized as neutral passage-keepers

### Year 234: The Great Flood
- Sewer backup flooded lower levels
- Les Canaux became heroes (knew alternate routes)
- ~2,000 dead

### Year 234: The Last Mediterranean Expedition (La Gare du Sud)
- Final successful contact with Mediterranean settlements
- What they found is classified
- No contact since
