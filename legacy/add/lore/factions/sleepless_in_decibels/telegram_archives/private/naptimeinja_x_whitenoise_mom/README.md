Collegial warmth between two exhausted parents who understood each other's desperation. Marie was more confident in her science than Marc was in his. She assured him The Hush was safe. She believed it.

## The Mutation

Marie discovered the Hush-2 mutation on October 8, 2034 — six days before activation. She noted an anomaly in spread rates. She planned to investigate after the satellites activated.

She never got the chance. Her last message to the Sounding Five channel:

> "It wasn't supposed to do this. The virus mutated. It's in everyone. Silence is lethal now. 4 hours. I've calculated it. I can't reach my kids. The school went quiet at noon. I'm so sorry. Tell my—"

The message ends there.
