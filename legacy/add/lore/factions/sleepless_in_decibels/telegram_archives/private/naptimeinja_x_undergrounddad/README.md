## Relationship

The most morally complex relationship in the entire Telegram archive.

Henri founded The Unplugged as a half-serious prepper group. Marc secretly supported it through an anonymous account (@QuietDad_FR), ensuring the group would survive the apocalypse he was helping to create. Their public-facing relationship was cordial fellow members. Their private DMs reveal the moment Henri got too close to the truth.

---

## Why Henri's Caves Mattered

Henri didn't choose the Bresme caves randomly. His grandparents — **Georges and Madeleine Marchand** — were born and raised in Saint-Etienne-de-Chigny. They lived in a traditional troglodyte house (*La Demeure aux Tilleuls*) carved into the tuffeau limestone cliffs above the Bresme valley.

Henri spent every summer of his childhood there (1985-1998), exploring the caves with his grandfather. Georges taught him:
- Which caves connected to which
- Where underground water flowed
- How to navigate in darkness
- The old quarrymen's marks that showed safe passages

When @QuietDad_FR suggested The Unplugged needed a "real" shelter, Henri immediately thought of his grandparents' caves. He knew every tunnel, every chamber, every secret passage.

He just didn't know why he'd need that knowledge.

Marc did.

## The Critical Exchange

**November 22, 2032**

**@UndergroundDad to @NapTimeNinja:**
> "Marc, I don't want to pry. But some of us are noticing... patterns. The satellite launches. The virus research Marie mentioned once. Are you guys actually doing something? Should I be more worried about my caves?"

*Marc stares at the message for three hours.*

**@NapTimeNinja to @UndergroundDad (three hours later):**
> "Henri, just focus on your caves. Whatever happens, you'll be fine."

---

## Interpretation

That message haunted both men.

**To Henri:** It was cryptic, unsettling, and ultimately saved his life. He doubled down on preparations without understanding why.

**To Marc:** It was the closest he could come to a warning without revealing the conspiracy. He couldn't tell Henri the truth. He could only make sure Henri was ready.

---

## Post-Revelation

**September 15, 2034** — Marc drove to Lyon and revealed himself as @QuietDad_FR. He asked to formally join The Unplugged with his family.

Henri's response: "The drums. The cave depth. That was you."

Henri never asked why Marc knew what they'd need. Some truths are better left unspoken.

## Survival

- **Marc:** Survived with The Unplugged
- **Henri:** Survived initial evacuation, died Year 1 AS (heart condition)

They worked side by side in the caves. Henri never learned the full truth.
