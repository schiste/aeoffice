## Overview

"Sleepless in Decibels" was the private Telegram group founded in 2026 where exhausted parents vented about noise complaints. What started as a support community evolved into [Operation Lullaby](../../thesaurus.md#operation-lullaby) — the conspiracy that ended civilization.

This folder contains detailed lore about the group and its members.

## Tone Guidance

When writing Sleepless in Decibels content:

- **They were not villains.** They were exhausted, desperate, and convinced they were helping.
- **The humor is genuine.** Their jokes came from real friendship and shared suffering.
- **The tragedy is slow.** Show the gradual slide from venting to conspiracy.
- **Every member is a person.** Even the architects of apocalypse loved their children.

See [tone_guide.md](../../tone_guide.md) for full writing guidance.
