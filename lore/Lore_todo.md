Players need discoverable lore:

### Quest Narratives — Empty

### The Hero's Story — Undefined

- How did they get the Crystal?
- What faction connections do they start with?

### Virus Mechanism Revision
**Status:** DONE (December 16, 2025)

Revised virus mechanism in [the-truth.md](the-truth.md): Sound vibrations make the virus dormant.

**The new mechanism:**
1. Hush-2 lodges in brainstem auditory processing centers
2. Sound vibrations (via bone conduction and inner ear) keep the virus dormant
3. In silence, the virus activates and attacks neural tissue
4. Lethal damage accumulates over ~4 hours
5. The virus was originally designed to respond to acoustic stimulation — the mutation inverted this behavior
