## Purpose

This directory contains detailed information about the fictional (and real-world-adjacent) cultural elements of the pre-Silence world (2026-2034). These artifacts become:

- **Sacred relics** — Objects survivors preserved without understanding
- **Mythologized history** — Stories distorted through 300 years of oral tradition
- **Quest hooks** — Items players can discover that recontextualize the world
- **Comedic opportunities** — The gap between what things *were* and what survivors *think* they were

## Year 300 Interpretation Framework

When creating worldbuilding entries, consider:

### What Survived

| Medium | Survival Rate | Why |
|--------|---------------|-----|
| **Paper (waraq)** | Very High | 300-year archival paper was universal by 2034 |
| **Vinyl records** | High | Durable, no power needed to preserve |
| **Digital devices** | Low | Require power, corrupted over time |
| **Oral tradition** | Variable | Distorts but persists |
| **Physical objects** | Variable | Depends on material, storage conditions |

### How It's Misunderstood

| Original Context | Year 300 Interpretation |
|------------------|------------------------|
| Corporate logo | Religious symbol |
| Advertisement slogan | Ancient proverb |
| Product manual | Sacred instruction text |
| Celebrity photo | Depiction of a god or hero |
| Warning label | Prophetic curse |
| Receipt | Census record |
