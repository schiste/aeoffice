## Thematic Irony Notes

The pre-Silence years are rich with dramatic irony for narrative use:

**Communication Milestones Celebrated Before Communication Ends:**
- 100th anniversary of NBC, first TV demonstration, first talkies
- 50th anniversary of first mobile phone call, CNN, MTV
- All celebrated just before the Filter makes communication impossible

**Warnings Ignored:**
- Orwell's "1984" anniversary in the year it happens
- Terminator's AI warnings just before the cascade
- Blade Runner's dystopia considered "optimistic"
- Climate scientists noting Dust Bowl parallels

**Music Culture at Its Peak:**
- Massive anniversaries: Thriller, Rumours, Saturday Night Fever, Back in Black
- Record festival attendance in 2034
- Fête de la Musique largest ever — the night before sound dies

**Pop Culture Artifacts for NPCs:**
- Characters born in these years might have anniversary merchandise
- Archive caches could contain commemorative items
- "My grandmother had a 50th anniversary Thriller vinyl" type stories
