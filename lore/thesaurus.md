## Core Concepts

### The Silence / The Deadly Silent
The apocalyptic event of October 14, 2034, when the satellite dampening field activated globally. "The Deadly Silent" is the common name (dark humor inversion of "silent but deadly"); "The Silence Event" is the formal [Archive](#the-archive) designation. See [timeline.md](timeline.md#the-cascade-october-14-2034) for hour-by-hour details.

### The Filter
The satellite network of 340 orbital platforms that generates the global sound-dampening field. Suppresses airborne sound propagation (20Hz-15kHz). Cannot be shut down—kill codes required ground-based radio transmission, which no longer propagates. Created by [The Sounding Five](#the-sounding-five) as part of [Operation Lullaby](#operation-lullaby). See [the-truth.md](the-truth.md#the-plan-as-designed) for technical specs.

### The Hush / Hush-2
- **The Hush (Original):** Synthetic retrovirus designed by [@WhiteNoise_Mom](the-truth.md#key-conspirators-the-sounding-five) to induce mild hyperacusis. Intended to be benign.
- **Hush-2:** The mutated variant that emerged October 8-10, 2034. Attacks brainstem auditory processing centers. Requires constant auditory input to regulate autonomic functions. 4-hour silence = death for most humans. See [timeline.md](timeline.md#the-mutation-october-8-10-2034) for mutation details.

### Crystals / Resonance Anomalies
Naturally-occurring mineral formations (high quartz/silicon content) that create localized sound bubbles. Formed at standing wave nodes over 50-200 years post-Silence. Cannot be artificially created. ~200-400 exist worldwide; ~80 controlled by settlements. **Not part of the conspiracy**—an emergent phenomenon. See [the-truth.md](the-truth.md#the-crystals-resonance-anomalies) for science and [timeline.md](timeline.md#the-first-crystal-2041) for discovery history.

### The Bubble
The safe zone around a [Crystal](#crystals--resonance-anomalies) where sound propagates normally. Size depends on acoustic energy fed to the Crystal. The player's primary domain to expand and protect. See [specifications.md](../mechanics/specifications.md) for bubble mechanics.

## People & Roles

### Resilients
Humans carrying the Hush-2.7r variant mutation. Have 24-hour tolerance to silence (vs. standard 4 hours). Prevalence: ~1 in 50,000. Often exploited as scouts by settlements. The Hero is a Resilient. First documented case: [Ayo Okonkwo](timeline.md#the-resilient-emergence-c-2201) (c. 2201). See [the-truth.md](the-truth.md#the-heros-resistance) for narrative implications.

### The Sounding Five
The five conspirators who created [Operation Lullaby](#operation-lullaby). Originally called "The Founding Five" until @DeciBella made a pun in March 2028 that stuck. Four died October 15-19, 2034; one survived with [The Unplugged](#the-unplugged). See [the-truth.md](the-truth.md#key-conspirators-the-sounding-five) for full profiles and [timeline.md](timeline.md#the-sounding-five--final-messages) for their final days.
- @NapTimeNinja (Marc Delacroix) — ESA architect — **survived with The Unplugged** (secretly guided them since 2032 via anonymous account @QuietDad_FR)
- @BassDrop_Dad (Michael Torres) — SpaceX engineer
- @WhiteNoise_Mom (Marie Fontaine) — Pasteur virologist
- @DeciBella (Dr. Sarah Chen) — MIT acoustics PhD
- @CribRocker (Jonathan Reeves) — venture capitalist

### The Unplugged
Survivalist splinter group from "[Sleepless in Decibels](#sleepless-in-decibels)" Telegram group. Founded December 2031 by @UndergroundDad (Henri Marchand). Prepared cave shelters across Europe. Their descendants populate the [Survivor Cave](#the-survivor-cave)—unaware their ancestors knew the conspirators. See [the-truth.md](the-truth.md#the-unplugged-the-survivors-who-caused-it) for full history and [timeline.md](timeline.md#2032) for preparation details.

### Drummers
Generic term for anyone maintaining rhythm/sound in a settlement. Critical survival role. Organized into shifts to ensure 24/7 coverage. See [naming_conventions.md](naming_conventions.md#people) for naming patterns.

## Factions

### The Choir
Religious faction (founded c. 2071). Believe [The Silence](#the-silence--the-deadly-silent) is divine punishment and [Crystals](#crystals--resonance-anomalies) are gifts from God. Know nothing about the conspiracy. Main settlement: Harmony's Rest (former Slovenia). See [timeline.md](timeline.md#the-choir-c-2071) for founding and [the-truth.md](the-truth.md#factions--the-truth) for what they know.

### The Engineers
Techno-rationalists (founded c. 2081). Suspect satellites are artificial; have partial schematics from ESA facility. Believe [Crystals](#crystals--resonance-anomalies) can be reverse-engineered and satellites shut down. Main settlement: The Foundry (former Germany). See [timeline.md](timeline.md#the-engineers-c-2081) for founding.

### The Silent
Radical adaptationists (founded c. 2101 by [Erasmus Vane](timeline.md#the-silent-c-2101)). Know about the virus mutation. Believe humanity should evolve to survive silence rather than fight it. Some practice "silence training." Have destroyed [Crystals](#crystals--resonance-anomalies). See [timeline.md](timeline.md#the-silent-c-2101) for founding myth.

### The Resonants
Crystal networkers (founded c. 2131). Split from [The Engineers](#the-engineers). Believe a network of connected [Crystals](#crystals--resonance-anomalies) can end The Silence forever. First successful relay: Year 2146. Main settlement: The Resonance (former Switzerland). See [timeline.md](timeline.md#the-resonants-c-2131) for history.

### The Archive
Information brokers (founded c. 2051). Control the [Telegram logs](#sleepless-in-decibels) and know almost everything about the conspiracy. Sell fragments of truth to all sides. Neutral—officially. "Knowledge is power; truth is currency." Main settlement: Archive Prime (former Switzerland). See [timeline.md](timeline.md#the-archive-c-2051) for founding and [the-truth.md](the-truth.md#the-telegram-logs) for what they control.

## Locations

### The Base
The player's primary settlement, built around their [Crystal](#crystals--resonance-anomalies). Expandable through construction and upgrades. See [specifications.md](../mechanics/specifications.md) for mechanics.

### The Survivor Cave
Cave settlement near the Hero's Base (6 tiles / ~24km). Descendants of [The Unplugged](#the-unplugged). One of the original prepared shelters (likely Ardèche Cave Complex branch). Hold artifacts that could reveal the truth. See [the-truth.md](the-truth.md#the-survivor-cave-descendants-of-the-the-unplugged) for full details and [specifications.md](../mechanics/specifications.md#341-crew-intake-survivor-cave--vibes-draft) for recruitment mechanics.

### The Frontier
The edge of explored/safe territory. Beyond lies uncharted zones with potential [Crystals](#crystals--resonance-anomalies), dangers, and discoveries.

### Safe Spots
Temporary or permanent outposts established outside the main [Bubble](#the-bubble). Require continuous sound generation to remain viable. See [specifications.md](../mechanics/specifications.md) for construction rules.

## Game Mechanics Terms

### Tuning
The prestige/reset mechanic. Represents a fundamental recalibration. Some progress persists; most resets. See [specifications.md](../mechanics/specifications.md) for full rules.

### Resonance
Meta-progression currency earned through [Tuning](#tuning). Factors include [Frontier](#the-frontier) expansion, exploration depth, time played, and active achievements.

### Bassline / Chorus / Harmonics
Resource tiers (placeholder names). Represent processed materials from expeditions. See [specifications.md](../mechanics/specifications.md) for production chains.

### Crafting Tiers (TBD)
Crafting tiers are intentionally deferred and will be redesigned alongside itemization/gear.

### Vibes
Settlement morale/capacity resource. Affected by Bunks, overcrowding, and crew management. Negative Vibes impose efficiency penalties. See [specifications.md](../mechanics/specifications.md#341-crew-intake-survivor-cave--vibes-draft) for full formula.

### Attuned / Detuned
- **Attuned:** Active, functional (buildings, tech, perks)
- **Detuned:** Inactive, requiring re-attunement cost to restore (post-[Tuning](#tuning))

## Historical Terms

### Operation Lullaby
The conspiracy's internal codename for their plan to create "a quieter world." Components: [The Filter](#the-filter) (satellites) + [The Hush](#the-hush--hush-2) (virus). See [the-truth.md](the-truth.md#the-conspiracy-operation-lullaby) for full plan.

### Sleepless in Decibels
The Telegram group founded June 21, 2026 ([Fête de la Musique](#fête-de-la-musique)) by Marc Delacroix (@NapTimeNinja). Started as exhausted-parent support group; became the conspiracy's origin. Grew to 8,000+ members by 2034. Server logs survive in [Archive](#the-archive)-controlled bunker. See [timeline.md](timeline.md#2026) for founding and [the-truth.md](the-truth.md#the-telegram-group-sleepless-in-decibels) for overview.

### Fête de la Musique
France's national music day (June 21). Amateur musicians flood streets until 2am. The ironic founding date of the anti-noise conspiracy. See [timeline.md](timeline.md#2026) for the founding night.
