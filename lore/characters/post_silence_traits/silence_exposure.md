# Silence Exposure

> **Category:** Physical & Health
>
> **Template Field:** `Silence Exposure`
>
> **Dialog Impact:** Experience with danger, trauma, changed perspective

---

## Overview

Exposure to the Silence—the lethal absence of sound beyond protective areas—is the most defining experience in the post-Silence world. Those who have survived it are marked.

---

## Exposure Levels

### Never

**Definition:** Always protected. Has never experienced the Silence directly.

**Characteristics:**
- May be naive about danger
- Theoretical understanding only
- Hasn't been tested
- Comfortable, possibly complacent
- Common in protected settlements

**Dialog Markers:**
```
"I've never been outside the bubble."
"I know it's dangerous. I've been told."
"I can't imagine what it's like."
"I'm careful. I stay protected."
"Why would I risk it?"
```

**Story Implications:**
- May underestimate threats
- Could panic if exposed
- Innocence may be strength or weakness
- Growth potential through exposure

---

### Close Call

**Definition:** Experienced the edge of silence. Felt it approaching. Escaped in time.

**Characteristics:**
- Knows the fear viscerally
- Respectful of danger
- May be traumatized or toughened
- Has a "close call" story
- Changed but not transformed

**Dialog Markers:**
```
"I felt it. The edge of nothing."
"My ears rang for days after."
"I ran. I've never run like that."
"Close enough to know I never want to be closer."
"It's not just quiet. It's hungry."
```

**Story Implications:**
- Credible fear without severe change
- Valuable perspective
- May be more cautious
- Or may be more cavalier (survived once)

---

### Survivor

**Definition:** Was caught outside protection. Experienced true Silence. Survived with damage.

**Characteristics:**
- Permanent effects possible
- Knows what Silence feels like
- May have physical damage (hearing, etc.)
- Psychologically marked
- Rare and noteworthy

**Dialog Markers:**
```
"I heard nothing. Then I couldn't hear anything."
"The Silence got in. Part of me is still there."
"I don't remember escaping. Just waking up."
"Some things you don't survive. You just don't die."
"Don't ask me what I saw. I can't tell you."
```

**Story Implications:**
- Heavily marked by experience
- May have PTSD equivalent
- Valuable but damaged
- Survivor guilt possible

---

### Multiple Exposures

**Definition:** Several exposures over time. Either very lucky or very skilled.

**Characteristics:**
- Rare individual
- May be scout, rescue worker
- Either damaged or remarkably tough
- Community relies on them
- Possibly touched by something

**Dialog Markers:**
```
"Three times. I keep count."
"Each time I learn something. Stay longer."
"They say it gets easier. It doesn't."
"I know the Silence better than anyone. Wish I didn't."
"Maybe I'm lucky. Or maybe it's saving me for something."
```

**Story Implications:**
- Exceptional individual
- May be developing Resilience
- High value, high suspicion
- Walking between worlds

---

### Resilient

**Definition:** Can withstand extended Silence exposure. Fundamentally changed. Very rare.

**Characteristics:**
- Can survive outside for extended periods
- Permanently altered
- Viewed with awe and fear
- May feel inhuman
- Community relies on but distrusts

**Dialog Markers:**
```
"The Silence doesn't touch me. Not like it does you."
"I've been out for days. Alone."
"I don't know what I am anymore."
"They need me. They don't trust me."
"The Silence... sings. You wouldn't understand."
```

**Story Implications:**
- Major character status
- Fundamental mystery
- Community's relationship is complicated
- Represents humanity's edge

## Exposure Effects

### Physical Effects

| Exposure Level | Possible Physical Effects |
|----------------|--------------------------|
| Never | None |
| Close Call | Temporary hearing issues, headaches |
| Survivor | Hearing damage, chronic pain, sensory changes |
| Multiple | Cumulative damage or strange adaptation |
| Resilient | Unknown changes, heightened senses? |

### Psychological Effects

| Exposure Level | Possible Psychological Effects |
|----------------|-------------------------------|
| Never | Possible complacency |
| Close Call | Healthy fear, possible anxiety |
| Survivor | Trauma, flashbacks, changed worldview |
| Multiple | Complex relationship with danger |
| Resilient | Fundamental identity questions |

---

## Exposure and Community

How the community views different exposure levels:

| Exposure Level | Community Perception |
|----------------|---------------------|
| Never | Normal, protected, nothing special |
| Close Call | Story worth telling, respected |
| Survivor | Pitied or respected, marked |
| Multiple | Valuable, watched carefully |
| Resilient | Essential but frightening, other |
