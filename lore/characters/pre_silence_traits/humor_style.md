# Humor Style

> **Category:** Personal Traits
>
> **Template Field:** `Humor Style`

## Overview

Humor reveals personality, builds bonds, and serves as coping mechanism. For Sleepless in Decibels members, humor style affects how they handle desperation and connect with others.

---

## Styles

### Dry / Deadpan

**Definition:** Understatement, no smile, waits for the penny to drop.

**Characteristics:**
- Delivered without affect
- May be mistaken for serious
- Rewards the attentive listener
- Subtle and sophisticated
- Can seem cold to some

**Sample Jokes:**
```
"The good news is I got three hours of sleep. The bad news is I need eight."

"My neighbor got a drum kit. So that's nice."

"I've tried everything. Earplugs, white noise, prayer, divorce lawyers."

[After extensive discussion] "So, we're fine then."
```

**In Group Chat:**
- Easily misread as serious
- May need "/s" or clarification
- Builds cult following
- Those who get it, get it

---

### Self-Deprecating

**Definition:** Jokes at own expense, disarming, builds rapport through vulnerability.

**Characteristics:**
- "I'm a mess" energy
- Makes others comfortable
- Can mask real pain
- Disarms tension
- May be fishing for reassurance

**Sample Jokes:**
```
"I'm pretty sure I put the coffee in the fridge and the milk on the counter. Third time this week."

"My parenting strategy is 'survive.' Low bar, still failing."

"I tried to explain the problem to my mom. She suggested I try sleeping more. Thanks, mom."

"I'm one bad night away from a Lifetime movie."
```

**In Group Chat:**
- Relatable content
- Easy to like
- May be genuinely struggling
- Others feel safe sharing

---

### Observational

**Definition:** Points out absurdities, "have you noticed" comedy, finds humor in patterns.

**Characteristics:**
- Notices what others miss
- "Isn't it weird how..."
- Finds universal experiences
- Often builds slowly
- Feels true

**Sample Jokes:**
```
"Has anyone noticed we've all collectively agreed that 5am counts as morning? It doesn't."

"There's a special kind of person who decides to learn drums at 40. I've met mine."

"We're all in this group because we believed in 'quiet enjoyment.' The biggest lie in any lease."

"Sleep when the baby sleeps. Clean when the baby cleans. Lose your mind when the baby loses your mind."
```

**In Group Chat:**
- Gets "lol same" reactions
- Builds community through shared experience
- Often quotable
- May become group's voice

---

### Dark / Gallows

**Definition:** Jokes about death, danger, terrible things. Coping mechanism.

**Characteristics:**
- "I'll laugh or I'll cry" energy
- May shock or offend
- Actually serious underneath
- Bonds those who share it
- Marks desperation

**Sample Jokes:**
```
"On the bright side, when civilization collapses from this noise weapon we're apparently inventing, at least I'll finally sleep."

"I Googled 'is it illegal to destroy a subwoofer' and now I'm on a list."

"Sleep deprivation is technically torture. I'm being tortured. This is fine."

"My baby slept through the night once. I thought he'd died. I almost preferred the screaming."
```

**In Group Chat:**
- May need content warnings
- Bonds tight with those who get it
- Signals serious distress
- Can be cathartic or concerning

---

### Slapstick / Physical

**Definition:** Pratfalls, exaggeration, broad physical comedy.

**Characteristics:**
- Big gestures
- Exaggerated descriptions
- Performance mode
- Universal appeal
- May seem juvenile to some

**Sample Jokes:**
```
"I'm so tired I walked into a door. Full speed. Made a noise. Woke the baby."

"You know that moment when you finally get the baby down and you're tiptoeing out and your knee CRACKS like a gunshot? Comedy gold."

*exaggerated zombie walk* "This is me. This is all of us."

"I fell asleep standing up in the shower. Woke up when I fell. Bruise is healing."
```

**In Group Chat:**
- GIFs and reaction images
- Visual descriptions
- Easy to appreciate
- Light relief

---

### Wordplay

**Definition:** Puns, double meanings, language jokes.

**Characteristics:**
- Groans welcome
- Language-focused
- Quick wit
- Not for everyone
- May be compulsive

**Sample Jokes:**
```
"I'm absolutely exhausted. Un-rest-ed, if you will."

"My neighbor's bass is so loud I can feel it in my bones. That's humerus. Wait, no it's not."

"We're all sleepless in [city]. Sleepless in Decibels. That's our thing now."

"My partner said I'm becoming 'noise-obsessed.' I told them that's sound advice."
```

**In Group Chat:**
- Quick exchanges
- May be groaned at
- Builds own subculture
- Dad joke energy

---

### Dad Jokes

**Definition:** Groan-worthy, family-friendly, corny.

**Characteristics:**
- Wholesome
- Predictable format
- Makes kids roll eyes
- Comfortable and safe
- Marks parental identity

**Sample Jokes:**
```
"I told my kid we're moving to a quieter place. They asked 'where?' I said 'you'll see.'" *finger guns*

"My sleep schedule is so bad, I put it on the naughty list."

"What do you call a sleeping baby? A miracle. Thank you, I'll be here all week. Because I can't leave."

"Why did the parent cross the road? To get away from the bass."
```

**In Group Chat:**
- Safe humor
- Marks "I'm a parent" identity
- Easy entry point
- May seem out of touch with severity

---

### None Apparent

**Definition:** Doesn't joke. May not recognize humor. Takes things literally.

**Characteristics:**
- Straight-faced always
- May miss jokes
- Others unsure how to interact
- Could be cultural, neurological, or choice
- Not humorless—just private

**Sample Interactions:**
```
OTHER: "I'm so tired I could die."
CHARACTER: "That's concerning. Have you seen a doctor?"

OTHER: [makes joke]
CHARACTER: "I don't understand what's funny about that."

CHARACTER: "The situation is serious. I don't see how joking helps."
```

**In Group Chat:**
- May not engage with humor threads
- Brings conversation back to substance
- Others may feel judged
- Could be deeply serious about everything
