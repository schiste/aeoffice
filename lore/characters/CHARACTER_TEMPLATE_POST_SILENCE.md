**Lineage Options:**
- `Founding Line` — Descended from original Unplugged survivors; carries old knowledge, old guilt
- `First Generation` — Family joined caves in early years; "almost founders"
- `Refugee Blood` — Ancestors arrived seeking shelter; still proving worth generations later
- `Bubble-Born` — Family has always lived within Crystal protection; knows nothing else
- `Nomad Strain` — Family traveled between settlements; mixed traditions, adaptable
- `Outcast Redeemed` — Ancestor was exiled, family worked back into community
- `Unknown` — Orphan, foundling, or deliberately obscured history

**Settlement Type:**
- `Cave Dweller` — Comfortable underground; claustrophilia; distrusts open sky
- `Crystal Settlement` — Raised in bubble; understands resonance intuitively
- `Coastal` — Knows tides, waves as sound; different rhythm sense
- `Industrial Ruin` — Grew up with machine sounds; practical, mechanical thinking
- `Nomadic` — No fixed home; reads terrain, people, danger quickly
- `Frontier` — Edge of known territory; self-reliant, suspicious, resourceful

**Generation from Silence:**
- `9th-10th (Elder)` — Grandparents knew people who remembered Before; carries oral history
- `11th-12th (Established)` — "Before" is ancient history but feels real through stories
- `13th+ (Young)` — "Before" is mythology; the bubble is the entire world
