## Description

The main public channel for "Sleepless in Decibels" — a Telegram support group for exhausted parents driven to the edge by noise pollution. What started as a place to vent about neighbors, festivals, and sleepless nights became the largest noise-complaint community in Europe.

Members shared stories, commiserated, and fantasized about a quieter world. Most had no idea that five of their fellow members were actually building the technology to make that fantasy a reality.

## Channel Tone

Darkly humorous venting. Members competed to share the worst noise stories. Running jokes about "quiet hours" being meaningless and landlords being useless. Genuine emotional support mixed with gallows humor.

The group had no idea they were the unwitting cover for a conspiracy that would end civilization.
