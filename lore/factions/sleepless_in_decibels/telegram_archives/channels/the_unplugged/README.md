## Description

A sub-channel of Sleepless in Decibels for members interested in emergency preparedness. Founded by @UndergroundDad (Henri Marchand) after a public thread asked "What if something goes wrong?"

What started as half-joking "prepper" discussions became a sophisticated survival network with:
- 5 major cave complexes across Europe
- Supplies for 500+ people for 18+ months
- Tested evacuation protocols
- Drums. So many drums.

Unknown to Henri and most members, one of their most helpful contributors (@QuietDad_FR) was actually @NapTimeNinja using an anonymous account to ensure The Unplugged would survive what he was helping to create.

## Channel Tone

Cheerfully paranoid. Members joked about being "crazy preppers" while methodically building survival infrastructure. The channel motto: "Paranoia is just preparedness with anxiety."

The drums were included "for psychological well-being." No one knew they would be the only thing keeping everyone alive.

---

## Legacy

The Unplugged's descendants form the core of survivor settlements 300 years later. They tell stories of "the founders who saw it coming." They don't know one of those founders helped cause it.
